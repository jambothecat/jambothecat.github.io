Music Around the World

Where does popular music really come from in the world?

When we think of popular music, we think of whatever is catchy at the moment. However, do we consider where the great minds behind each piece of music is from? Billboard Magazine, or simply Billboard, is known for keeping track of trends and the like within the music industry. It expanded from here in the United States to all across the globe, keeping track of all of the latest trends in music. One of its most popular charts is the Billboard Global Top 200 that dates back to all the way from 1958. Observing where these charting artists are from may tell us more about underlying trends as we go from decade to decade...

Value, Data, Feedback

The bubble map identifies where exactly each artist is from from each decade. The bar graph summarizes the overall and total trend in the data. For data, we used the Billboard Global Top 200 and MusicBrainz. Based on the feedback we received from classmates, the story and message itself seemed to present quite clearly when the prototypes were shown. However, clarity was needed in what the individual visualizations were representing, and the most common suggestion was to add interactive legends or keys of some kind in order to label the visualizations more clearly. When receiving feedback from Professor Macneil, however, we were able to identify that our original idea on covering individual genres of music across time may not have been telling the strongest story. Instead, we changed our idea to instead cover the locations of individual artists across the Billboard 200 throughout the years and to observe its underlying trends.
